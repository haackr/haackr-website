At the recent GatsbyConf 2021, Gatsby announced a new service: hosting on Gatsby Cloud. Along with that announcment, they are currently running a promotion for the month of March where anyone who hosts their site on Gatsby Cloud (for free) can recieve a $15 swag coupon usable in the Gatsby store, so those of us who attended GatsbyConf this year, but sadly missed out on getting any free swag still have an opportunity.
